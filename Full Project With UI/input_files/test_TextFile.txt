The Department of Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) at the University of Chittagong is one of the leading departments in Bangladesh, known for its academic excellence and research contributions. Established in 2001, the department offers undergraduate and graduate programs in computer science and engineering, with a focus on developing skilled professionals in the field. The department has a highly qualified faculty who are dedicated to providing quality education and fostering innovation.

Students in the CSE department engage in hands-on learning through various labs, projects, and internships that prepare them for real-world challenges. The curriculum is regularly updated to include the latest advancements in technology, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the rapidly evolving tech industry. Research is a key aspect of the department, with faculty and students involved in projects related to artificial intelligence, machine learning, data science, cybersecurity, and software engineering.

The department maintains strong links with the industry and organizes workshops, seminars, and conferences to keep students and faculty members up to date with the latest trends and technologies. The CSE department of the University of Chittagong is committed to creating a collaborative and dynamic learning environment that nurtures both academic and professional growth.
